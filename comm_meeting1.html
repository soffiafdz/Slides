Alzheimer's Disease

High Prevalence

High economic cost

Biological affliction precedes symptoms by decades

Hippocampus

  • Viable proxy for structural and functional integrity
  • High variability in volume across populations

Hippocampal-to-Ventricle Ratio

  • Ventricular enlargement is a marker of brain atrophy
  • Schoemaker, et al (2019) used manual segmentation to compute the ratio between prehippocampal CSF and hippocampal volume
  • It showed significantly stronger negative associations with age and was a better predictor of memory compared to standard hippocampal volume

Hypotheses

Hypotheses

  1. HVR can be reliably calculated using automatic segmentation (T1w MRI)
  2. HVR will predict progression to dementia better than other hippocampal related methods
  3. There will be significant differences in HVR spatiotemporal curves between:
    • Dementia-progressors v No-cognitive-decline;
    • Amyloid+ v Amyloid-;
    • APOE4+ v APOE4-;
