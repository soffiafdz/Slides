Alzheimer's Disease

High Prevalence

High economic cost

Biological affliction precedes symptoms by decades

Hippocampus

  • Viable proxy for structural and functional integrity
  • High variability in volume across populations

Hippocampal-to-Ventricle Ratio

  • Ventricular enlargement is a marker of brain atrophy
  • Schoemaker, et al (2019) used manual segmentation to compute the ratio between prehippocampal CSF and hippocampal volume
  • It showed significantly stronger negative associations with age and was a better predictor of memory compared to standard hippocampal volume

Hypotheses

  1. HVR can be reliably calculated using automatic segmentation (T1w MRI)
  2. HVR will predict progression to dementia better than other hippocampal related methods
  3. There will be significant differences in HVR spatiotemporal curves between:
    • Dementia-progressors v No-cognitive-decline;
    • Amyloid+ v Amyloid-;
    • APOE4+ v APOE4-;

Research Plan

AIM 1

Build an automatic tool that can segment the Hippocampus and surrounding Ventricles from MRI

  • Available labels from Schoemaker, et al.
  • Develop an automatic segmentation tool
  • Compare the performance of different segmentation techniques:
    • Patch-based segmentation; Coupé, et al. 2011
    • Multi-atlas label fusion; Coupé, et al. 2011
    • Deep-Learning: Coupé, et al. 2011

AIM 2

Model spatiotemporal curves of development of HVR at the different stages of AD and healthy aging

  • Validate HVR in large open databases (healthy & clinical)
  • Access to several databases:
    UKBB, ADNI, AIBL, Prevent-AD and CIMA-Q
  • Linear mixed effect models
    Control for sociodemographic and MRI covariates
  • Expected different rates of hippocampal deterioration between pathological and healthy aging

AIM 3

Evaluate HVR's predicting performance for MCI to dementia progression

  • Train different ML classifiers:
    Raw HC volume v Normalized HC volume v SNIPE v HVR
    • Classify AD clinical stage (CN v MCI V AD)
    • Predict MCI progression to dementia (sMCI vs pMCI)
  • Trained and tested on the available clinical datasets

AIM 4

Explore viability of HVR as early biomarker, identifying progression from SCD to MCI and/or AD

  • Subjective Cognitive Decline Cohort (SCD)
    ADNI, AIBL, Prevent-AD, CIMA-Q
  • In CN subjects, compare HVR:
    subjective memory complaints v no complaints
  • See if it's possible to predict:
    • future measurabe cognitive impairment
    • clinical progression to MCI/AD

Significance

  • Tool for automatic tissue segmentation
  • Validation of Hippocampal to Ventricle Ratio in normative and clinical samples
  • Better clinical biomarker for dementia
  • More effective identification of the early stages of AD
